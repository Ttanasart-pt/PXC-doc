Bend the surface by a predefined shape.

## Properties

### <junc surface in>
Surface to bend

### <junc type>
Type of the bend

### Arc

#### <junc axis>
Main axis of the arc

#### <junc amount>
Arc curvature

### Wave

#### <junc axis>
Axis of the wave

#### <junc amount>
The size/amplitude of the wave

#### <junc scale>
The scale/frequency of the wave

#### <junc shift>
The offset/phase of the wave
